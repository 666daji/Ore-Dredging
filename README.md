## Cimelia

In this mod, when breaking various rocks, in addition to dropping vanilla ores, there is a **chance to drop even more precious and exciting treasures**. We call them **Cimelia**.

Each cimelia has **one or more unique and powerful functions**, and we will continue to update with more interesting content!

## Crushed Block

The mod adds **reasonable and abundant drops** to rocks, hiding mysterious relics within!

Currently, **all rocks and ores in the Overworld** will drop various ores and treasures according to their own characteristics.

### 📋 Drop Mechanics

Each time you mine a rock, you have a **25% chance to shatter it**. When shattered, you will obtain:

- **Rock Pile**
- **Possible ores**

## Gravel Piles
**Rock Piles** are the main product after rocks are shattered in this mod. They come in **five different block models**, adding variety and making them suitable for decoration.

### 🧱 Crafting and Uses

- Except for **Sandstone Rock Piles**, all other rock piles can be used to craft corresponding **stone bricks**, or be broken down into **four corresponding pebbles**.
- Rock piles and pebbles also **generate naturally in the world**, decorating your environment.


Most rocks will yield corresponding pebbles. They can be used not only for building decoration but also:

- As a substitute for **flint** in crafting arrows.
- As **projectiles** to deal damage to enemies.
- Each type of pebble has **different characteristics**: some fly faster, some deal more damage, some have a shorter attack cooldown—wait for you to discover!

> 💥 The projectile damage of pebbles is affected by the **Strength effect**: each level of Strength increases the damage by 1 point. They will be your trusty companion in the early stages of mining!

## Miner Bundle
To handle the large number of drops, this mod adds **three tiers of Mineral Pouches**, which are extremely practical!

### 🎒 Crafting and Upgrade Path

1. **Leather Mineral Pouch**
    - Crafting materials: Leather + String
    - Capacity: **4 stacks of items**
    - Mechanics same as vanilla bundle

2. **Chain Mineral Pouch**
    - Upgrade materials: Rabbit Hide + Chain
    - Capacity: Increases by an additional 4 stacks

3. **Phantom Mineral Pouch**
    - Upgrade materials: Phantom Membrane + Slimeball
    - Capacity: Increases by another 4 stacks

Three incredibly useful enchantments for miner bundle:

- **Convergence (Level I)**: Automatically compresses mineral nuggets in the pouch into corresponding raw ores or ingots.
- **Auto Picking (Level I)**: When you pick up mineral items from the ground, they are automatically stored into the pouch.
- **Expansion (Levels I–III)**: Each level increases the pouch's capacity by 0.5 times, and the effect stacks!
